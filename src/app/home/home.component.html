A New and Original Method for the Beginner Violinist

The Method

3 Decades of violin teaching have inspired Phil Holland to develop Divertimento Duets, a new and original approach to learning and teaching the violin.

This method is great fun for the student and an excellent and useful resource for the teacher. It breaks away from the traditional format of violin with piano accompaniment in order to encourage a more immersive experience between teacher and student, allowing for an organic and natural development of the student’s style.

Original Duets for Beginner Violin

These are completely new and original duets for the beginner violinist, structured to allow the student to gradually master new aspects of violin technique and musicianship.

No need for piano accompaniment

Not every teacher has access to a piano during lessons and not every teacher is overly confident about their ability to accompany at the piano. If this sounds familiar, then you will find Divertimento Duets ideal for you and your pupils.

The duet format also encourages complete interaction between teacher and pupil which helps develop:

  • a more fluid performance style
  • excellent intonation
  • a fully rounded technique

Perfect for one-on-one lessons, group teaching or just for fun

The two parts of each duet are of equal difficulty. As such it can be used as a violin teaching manual in:

  • one-on-one lessons
  • group teaching
  • first steps towards ensemble playing

By learning both parts of each duet, the student will gain a better understanding of how music fits together and feel a great sense of achievement and enjoyment.

Ideal first performance pieces

These duets are fun to play and enjoyable to listen to. This makes them ideal first performance pieces that would be well suited to both child and adult beginners.

Beautiful and original full colour illustrations

Lorna Romanenghi’s beautiful and unique illustrations really help to bring these duets to life on the page. Divertimento Duets is a violin book a student will love playing but also cherish.

MP3 recordings and teacher’s notes available as free downloads

Extensive teacher’s notes and MP3 recordings of all pieces in the book are available as free downloads from this website. The teacher’s notes may also be useful to parents and more mature beginners in order to give them insight into the learning process.

THE AUTHOR

Phil Holland (LRAM) graduated from the Royal Academy of Music, London in both violin performance and teaching. She has been teaching and writing for her students for the past 30 years. She founded and ran her own violin school, the Divertimento Academy which ran for over 20 years in the cities of Cremona and Crema in the North of Italy. She is now based in the West of Scotland.

THE ILLUSTRATOR

Lorna Romanenghi is an emerging Artist and Designer based in Scotland.

She spent her formative years in Italy but then relocated to Scotland where she was able to complete an HND in Art and Design at Ayrshire College and is currently completing her BD (hons) Degree at Duncan of Jordanstone College of Art and Design, Dundee.
