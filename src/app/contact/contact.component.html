FAQ

Is this book suitable for an absolute beginner?

Yes. Divertimento Duets, Volume 1 is aimed at a complete beginner.

The pieces are intended to be learnt sequentially in order to introduce in the correct order the various technical and musical aspects of developing a solid technique right from the word go.

 Is this book suitable for all age groups?

Yes. Divertimento Duets would be suitable for all ages from the very young to the mature beginner. They are not specifically aimed at any age group, but they are aimed at a beginner level.

The illustrations have also been specifically created to please all age groups.

 Can I use the Divertimento Duets method without a teacher?

If you are already learning the violin and want to use Divertimento Duets for fun to play with a friend, then the answer is a resounding Yes.

You can use these for pure enjoyment.

The pieces are great fun to play and very enjoyable to listen to and will give you hours of fun. If, on the other hand, you are an absolute beginner, I would never advise someone to try and learn without an experienced teacher to guide them through the many pitfalls of developing a sound violin technique.

However, a mature beginner will find that the very extensive teacher’s notes that can be downloaded for free to accompany the book, will be a great help to practising and give valuable insight into the learning process.

 Is this method compatible to use alongside other violin teaching methods?

Yes. Divertimento Duets can be used as the basis of a teaching/learning programme, but it can also be used as an extra teaching tool to be used alongside other methods.

It is also very useful for teachers who run group sessions for beginners and can be an extremely effective way of introducing the first steps towards ensemble playing.
