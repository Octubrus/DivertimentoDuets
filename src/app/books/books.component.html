3 Decades of violin teaching have inspired Phil Holland to develop Divertimento Duets, a New and Original approach to learning and teaching the violin.

Volume 1 is the first in the series, aimed at beginner level. These 23 original duets help the beginner gradually master new aspects of violin technique and musicianship while having fun and learning to play with others. There is no need for a piano accompaniment, making these perfect for one-on-one lessons, group teaching or just for fun.

The Divertimento Duets approach breaks away from the traditional violin/piano accompaniment format, instead presenting a fun and effective way of learning the violin that encourages complete interaction between teacher and pupil. This is not only a more immersive approach to violin learning, it also helps to develop the pupil’s ability to play with and listen to others from the very start. It is the perfect teaching tool for a teacher who does not have access to a piano.

“Divertimento Duets” is an invaluable publication in helping a beginner violinist on the road to musical achievement and development. It is also beautifully illustrated in full colour making it a joy to own and an extra incentive to get the music on the stand and start practising.
